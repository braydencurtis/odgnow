Pre-Trial

  • Assist in pre-trial preparation, including database creation and maintenance
  • Coordinate data, including video, documents, and demonstrative evidence; to ensure ease of use, search, and retrieval
  • Support in the preparation, creation, or modification of exhibits
  • Create witness video clips from video depositions for impeachment purposes and playback of testimony for unavailable witnesses
  • Custom professional video editing
  • Prepare trial and witness notebooks with electronic bate-stamp for court
  • Help paralegals, attorneys, and expert witnesses understand what presentation techniques work best at trial

Trial Presentation

  • Provide complete technical support for presentation systems and technology in the courtroom
  • Pre-load and present your case exhibits and video at trial using Trial Director and other trial technology software
  • Arbitration and Mock Trial complete support and presentation
  • Produce professional presentations: Opening & Closings, Timelines, etc.
  • Ensure appropriate equipment location for line of sight, lighting, and audio
